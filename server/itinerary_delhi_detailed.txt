#### General Information
- **National holidays**: No public holidays in Delhi during these dates. Christmas (Dec 25) and New Year’s Day (Jan 1) are upcoming but do not affect the itinerary.
- **Local transit**: Delhi Metro operates daily from **05:30 am to 23:30 pm**. Last train from New Delhi station to the Airport Express departs at 23:30. Use the **Yellow Line** (Airport Express) for airport transfers and the **Violet, Blue, and Magenta lines** for intra‑city travel.
- **Budget tip**: Metro fares range from ₹10‑₹60 per ride; Uber/Ola are ₹200‑₹500 for short trips. Carry a **N95/KN95 mask** for moderate‑to‑unhealthy AQI, a light sweater for cool mornings, and a cross‑body bag for valuables.

#### Safety & Practical Tips
- **Air quality**: Carry an N95/KN95 mask; avoid strenuous outdoor activity 07:00‑10:30 am & 17:00‑20:00 pm.
- **Dress code**: Modest clothing (shoulders & knees covered) for religious sites; remove shoes where required.
- **Pickpocket awareness**: Use a cross‑body bag, especially in crowded markets (Chandni Chowk, Dilli Haat).
- **Transport**: Prefer Metro for traffic‑free travel; use Uber/Ola for late‑night or off‑Metro routes.
- **Currency**: Carry some cash (₹500‑₹2000) for small vendors; most restaurants accept cards.
- **Emergency numbers**: Police 112, Ambulance 102, Fire 101.
